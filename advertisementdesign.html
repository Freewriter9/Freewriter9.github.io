advertisement design

Overview

Advertisement design competition for inter-university competition - fourth position out of 14 entries

Description

A major part of the IIT college life are the inter-hostel competition and advertisement competition is part of it. The problem statement required us to devise an ad campaign for an exchange app which lets users give their old things and get discounts on the brand new version of the same thing.

I as a part of a three member team devised the marketing strategy, the print ads, the billboard ad, the radio ad and the video ad. Conceptualized the advertising campaign of an e-commerce mobile app based on detailed market study and user analysis. We produced the first ever animated advertisement submission using 50+ illustrations; devised marketing strategies for print media and billboards
